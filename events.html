15th April, 2019 - (G)Astronomy Competition

Where: Giardini del DIFA, Navile
When: From 13:00 to 14:00
Who: Students, astronomers... everyone!
Why: To have fun
How: Read carefully the rules below!


Competition Rules:

  1. All entries must be homemade and must portray an astrophysical object (eg. planet, nebula, galaxy, ...)
  2. Each partecipant can present only one dish.
  3. The quantity should be sufficient for at least 15 people to taste it.
  4. Bring your item to the event location before 13:15.
  5. Please provide this information on a separate piece of paper: (a) Name of Product, (b) Name of the Astrophysical object, (c) Your Name.
